# Design Context

**Target Audience:** 
Family members, friends, Sudanese participants in the 2019 uprising, as well as NGOs and Civil Society Organizations (CSOs).

**Use Cases:** 
A digital memory. The primary action is reading the deeply moving personal stories and entries. Finding records, sharing memories.

**Brand Personality & Tone:**
Editorial, archival, monumental, and deeply textured. It should be respectful, reverent, somber but strong. It represents a physical archive turned digital—avoiding standard tech interfaces, favoring book-like typography, robust spacing, stark photography formatting, and paper/ink aesthetics.
